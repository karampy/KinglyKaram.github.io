Who is Bana?

Bana is a Kurdish rising pop artist based
in the Kurdistan region of Iraq. She made
her first appearence on the talent show
"Kurd Idol". That milestone marked her
first victory as an established singer.
Through her soothing voice and charming
looks, she changes what is a grim environment
to a world filled with color, love, and joy.
Just like the flowers on this page!
